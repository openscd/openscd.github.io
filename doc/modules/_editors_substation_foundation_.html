    • Defined in src/editors/substation/foundation.ts:23

    Moves the element edited by editor to the place before the next Child editor selected or to the end of the next Parent editor selected by mouse click or keyboard (space or enter key).

    The move action can be aborted by clicking on something other than a Child or Parent editor or by hitting the escape key on the keyboard.
